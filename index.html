Professional Electrician Services

Our certified electricians provide comprehensive electrical solutions for residential and commercial properties. We specialize in:

  • Complete home/office wiring with premium quality cables
  • Installation of all types of lighting fixtures including chandeliers, LED panels, and spotlights
  • Electrical panel upgrades and maintenance
  • Switchboard and socket installations with child safety features
  • Smart home automation solutions including lighting control and security systems
  • Safety inspections and compliance checks with detailed reports
  • 24/7 emergency electrical repairs with rapid response times

All our work comes with a 2-year warranty and uses only high-quality materials from trusted brands like Havells, Anchor, and Legrand.

False Ceiling Design Services

Transform your space with our innovative false ceiling solutions that combine aesthetics with functionality:

  • Gypsum ceiling designs (plain, curved, stepped, geometric patterns)
  • POP (Plaster of Paris) decorative ceilings with intricate designs
  • Wooden false ceilings for warm, natural appeal
  • Metal ceiling designs for contemporary spaces
  • Integrated lighting solutions (cove, recessed, spot lighting)
  • Acoustic ceiling solutions for noise reduction in offices and media rooms
  • Custom 3D and laser-cut designs for unique visual effects

Our designs are crafted to enhance your space's proportions, conceal imperfections, and create visual interest while improving acoustics and lighting distribution.

CCTV Camera Installation Services

Protect your property with our professional CCTV installation services:

  • HD and 4K camera systems for crystal clear footage day and night
  • Complete range of cameras: Indoor, outdoor, dome, bullet, and PTZ
  • Night vision cameras with infrared technology for 24/7 surveillance
  • Motion detection cameras with smartphone alerts
  • DVR and NVR systems with large storage capacity
  • Remote viewing via smartphone/computer from anywhere in the world
  • Complete wiring and concealment for neat installation
  • AI-powered surveillance with facial recognition and object detection

We offer solutions from top brands like Hikvision, CP Plus, and Dahua, tailored to your specific security needs with professional installation and maintenance packages.

Expert Carpentry Services

  • Custom furniture (beds, tables, chairs, sofas) designed to your specifications
  • Built-in wardrobes and storage solutions to maximize space
  • Kitchen cabinets and modular kitchens with premium finishes
  • Doors, windows, and frames with perfect fit and finish

Furniture Polish & Restoration Services

Bring new life to your wooden furniture with our professional polishing and restoration services:

  • Complete furniture polishing (French, matte, gloss finishes)
  • Scratch and dent repair with color matching
  • Wood stain removal and treatment
  • Antique furniture restoration preserving original character
  • Lamination and veneer work for surface renewal
  • Hardware replacement (handles, hinges, locks)
  • Protective coating application for long-lasting shine

We use eco-friendly, non-toxic polishes and techniques that enhance the natural beauty of wood while providing durable protection. Our restoration work can make old furniture look better than new while maintaining its original charm.
